Saat-Aath

Two-Player Indian Trick-Taking Game

How to Play Saat-Aath

Objective: Dealer needs 7+ tricks, Non-dealer needs 8+ tricks to win.

Trump Cards

• 7♥ is always the highest trump

• 7♠ is always the second highest trump

• Then trump suit cards: A, K, Q, J, 10, 9, 8

Card Rankings

Trump suit: 7♥, 7♠, A, K, Q, J, 10, 9, 8

Other suits: A, K, Q, J, 10, 9, 8

Playing Rules

• Must follow suit if possible (including 7♥ and 7♠ when trump is led)

• If can't follow suit, can play any card

• Higher card of led suit wins, or trump beats non-trump

• If different suits and no trump, first card wins

Setup

• Each player gets 5 cards in hand + 5 stacks on table

• Non-dealer chooses trump after seeing their hand

• Non-dealer leads first trick
